INDENTURE OF FAITH & INTENTION
of the BRITE POOL Ministerium of Empowerment
(Building Resources, Investing Together for Empowerment)

FAITH & FOUNDING PRINCIPLES
We hold these truths to be self-evident and spiritually affirmed:
A. That each man and woman is endowed by the Creator with inalienable rights,
including the right to associate, to contract, to create value, and to forgive and
be forgiven of trespasses.
B. That no entity—corporate, governmental, or artificial—holds dominion over the
living soul unless such dominion is voluntarily contracted.
C. That abundance is the natural state of the universe, and lack is a lie taught by
systems of control.
D. That true healing requires the freedom to choose one's path, modality, and
practitioner, without interference.
E. That trust, transparency, and truth are the governing principles of all BRITE
POOL operations.

ORDAINMENT
The Directing Chair Directors of the Board may or may not be ordained and shall serve
without requirement of bonds or commercial security of any nature.

MEMBERSHIP
A. Membership in BRITE POOL is by invitation and covenant, not public
registration. All members are known as Stewards or Fellows, entering by way of:
B. Conscious agreement to the values and vision set forth herein.
C. Acknowledgment of their private status as participants—not public consumers.
D. Respect for confidentiality, sovereignty, and lawful conduct.
E. All members must:
F. Affirm their status as living men or women of sound mind.
G. Enter by covenant, not contract.

GOVERNANCE
BRITE POOL affirms its standing as a Private, Ecclesiastical Ministerium under Divine
Law, Natural Law, and Lawful Equity.
The Ministerium shall be guided by a Council of Stewards, seated as a Board of Chairs,
each embodying a key pillar of the ministerium's mission (vision, healing, wealth,
governance, etc.). This council exists to serve as guardians of the Ministerium’s values,
vision, and lawful operations.
We do not consent to commercial regulation, corporate oversight, or statutory control.
We operate strictly within the realm of private association and ministerium.
This is not a commercial entity but a living covenantal vessel.
Our purpose is spiritual mission—not profit.
We do not serve shareholders but soul-holders.
All exchanges are rooted in mutual benefit, spiritual stewardship, and sovereign
responsibility.

BOARD CHAIR DIRECTORS
A. Upon formal establishment of the BRITE POOL Ministry of Empowerment, the
Founding Chair Directors may confirm and seat additional Chair Directors. Each
Director shall be a fiduciary steward within the Ministerial Council, holding equal
spiritual and administrative standing.
B. All Chair Directors carry equal authority. No Chair holds higher rank. Governance
shall be determined by consensus or supermajority agreement.
C. The Chair of Wealth & Management is entrusted with oversight of treasury functions
and may delegate roles necessary for bookkeeping and financial transparency—subject
to full board review.
D. All Chair Directors form the Full Ministerial Board, empowered to initiate and guide
all matters relating to the Association’s sacred mission.

E. Proxy voting is not permitted under any circumstance.
